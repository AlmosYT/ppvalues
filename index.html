PPvalues

What is PPvalues?

PPvalues is a politcal test that tries to gauge your opinion on the different issues of PixelPlace. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the eight values?

There are four independent axes - Political, Moderation, Rules, and Diplomacy - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

Reformist (Political)
Reformist players want to bring changes to the site: add new mods, change rules, or even the look of the site itself.

Conservative (Political)
Conservatives think that PixelPlace is good as it is and not a lot of change is needed.

Pro-Mod (Moderation)
Pro-Mod people support the current moderation team.

Anti-Mod (Moderation)
Anti-Mod people believe that the current moderation team is corrupt, in some way, and needs to be replaced.

Liberal (Rules)
These people believe that the rules are too strict, and that players should have the right to do anything they want, for example skipdot.

Anti-Dot (Rules)
Anti-Dot players believe that a clean-looking canvas they play on is more important then the liberty of the player.

Diplomatic (Diplomacy)
Diplomatic players believe that peace is sustainable, and that the factions should rather follow diplomatic principles for setteling disputes instead of agression and war.

Agressive (Diplomacy)
Agressive players believe that war can not be avoided in a land dispute, and its better to get it over with then negotiate.
